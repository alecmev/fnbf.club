What is Friday Night Battlefield, you might ask? Long story short, this is a weekly Battlefield 4 event, held on Fridays at 21:00 Central European Time. Many random people, with all kinds of backgrounds, join our TeamSpeak server and are split into two teams (or four, or six, or eight... we have plenty of space), first-come, first-served (those without competitive experience get special treatment, though). Then they play either 8v8 or 12v12, with veterans in charge, on several Conquest maps, and then they go home. That's it! No registration, no special planning, no bullshit.
